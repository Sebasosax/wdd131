About Us

Homemade flavor in every bite

In our small family restaurant, every dish is prepared with care and passion. We offer artisanal empanadas, juicy roasted chicken, delicious pizzas, sandwiches, and much more. Come and enjoy homemade flavors that will make you feel right at home!

Our Story

Our story began with Doña Betty, who, together with her children, started selling empanadas made with love. The youngest ones went door to door offering their delicacies to neighbors, while the daughters and Grandma Rosa helped in the kitchen.

Over time, more and more customers began to trust us and helped us grow, strengthening our unity as a family. Today, we want to share our love for food with even more people, offering homemade flavors that will make you feel at home.
